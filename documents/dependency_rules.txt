[SECTION] DEPENDENCY_SOURCE_DETECTION
Dependencies may originate from:

Vendor delivery
External system integration
Environment access
Contract approval
Infrastructure availability

[SECTION] DEPENDENCY_RISK_RELATION
Unresolved dependencies may create delivery risks.

If dependency delay impacts milestone timeline,
it must be recorded as Risk in RAIDD register.

[SECTION] DEPENDENCY_SUMMARY_RULE
Dependencies must appear in project summary reporting when:

milestone impact exists
external team coordination required
approval pending
