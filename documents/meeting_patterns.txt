Meeting transcripts must be analyzed to extract RAIDD items.

Indicators:

Risk:
possible delay
concern about
risk of failure

Issue:
system error
blocking problem
environment failure

Dependency:
waiting for access
dependent on vendor
requires external approval

Decision:
team agreed
scope approved
timeline confirmed

Assumption:
assuming compatibility
expected availability
planned resource support
