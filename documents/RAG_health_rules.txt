[SECTION] DELIVERY_HEALTH_CALCULATION
Project delivery health assessment must consider:

Risk volume
Issue severity
Dependency status
Resource availability
Vendor performance

[SECTION] RAID_HEALTH_IMPACT
High number of unresolved risks or issues may change delivery health status.

Examples:

Low risk + resolved issues → Green
Moderate risk + active issues → Amber
High risk + blocking issues → Red

[SECTION] SUMMARY_HEALTH_ALIGNMENT
Summary reports must reflect health indicators:

Scope alignment
Budget alignment
Schedule risk
Resource availability
RAIDD status
